Braixen Appreciation Page!

Hello everyone! I am StylusCat, and I'm here to show you my most favorite Pokemon of all time... Braixen!

Braixen is a fire type Pokemon introduced in the 6th generation Pokemon games, Which were Pokemon X and Y for the Nintendo 3DS family of systems.

Braixen is also famous for appearing in the "Pokemon the Series: XY" anime when Serena's Fennekin evolved into it.

Braixen has a stick in it's tail. Why? Well, it's actually a fire stick that it can use to attack! Braixen is also emotionally devoted to the stick, and would be devastated if anything happened to it!

What is Braixen's gender?

A Braixen can be male, or female. The chance of a Braixen being male is 87.5 percent, and female is 12.5 percent!

But of course, gender doesn't matter! a male Braixen is just as adorable as a female Braixen! :3


Why is Braixen so cute?

Well.. Braixen is a fox! and foxes are very cute creatures. Braixen also has a very appealing color pallete. the Yellow, Orange, White, Black, and Brown for the stick go together very well!

Braixen's personality is also very adorable. Braixen is shown to be very loyal and affectionate to their trainer. Going as far to mimic their own trainers actions!

Braixen is also popular in the furry community, for obvious reasons. furries love anthropomorphic Pokemon!


Braixen's Stick!!!

Braixen carries a stick in its tail. and it's on fire!!

Braixen keeps the stick inside of its tail. when Braixen takes the stick out of its tail, the friction causes it to light on fire.

Braixen's tail cannot be lit on fire since its fireproof. This means Braixen can put its stick back inside its tail to extinguish it!
