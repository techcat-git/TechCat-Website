Strategy Guide for Braixen Simulator

CHAPTER 1. RUNNING THE GAME:

To run the game, download the latest executable from the releases tab on the Braixen Simulator Github, or homepage.
Then, copy the file onto your MS-DOS computer and run it by running this command: "BRAIXEN.EXE".
If you are running a DOS based version of Windows, such as 95, or 98. you can double click the program and it'll open a DOS terminal and run the game for you.

CHAPTER 2. MAIN MENU:

When you reach the main menu of the game, press "1" on your keyboard, and press enter to start the game. you may also press "2" on your keyboard and press enter to reach the continue screen, where you can continue off on a specific chapter.
(Please note that this game has no saving capabilities, you will have to remember what chapter you left off on if you choose to exit the game.)

CHAPTER 3. INTRODUCTION SEQUENCE (PART 1):
After you read the prelude, you will be thrown into a battle like menu with three options. You are met with three options. the goal is to heighten your trust level to 100 to gain the trust of the Braixen.
1. Pet them
2. Throw a rock at them
3. Feed them

Trying to pet the Braixen when your trust level is under 25 will result in you being bitten. you must heighten your trust level before doing that.
You can up your trust level by selecting the "Feed them" option. this will heighten their trust level by 10. keep throwing berries until you dont have any left.
Since your trust level is now over 25, you can now pet the Braixen to finish off your trust level, and finally gain the trust of the wild Braixen!
(Note: chosing "Throw a rock at them" will reset your trust level and make the Braixen lose 10 health points. if you have no berries left, there's no way to recover from it.)

CHAPTER 3. INTRODUCTION SEQUENCE (PART 2):
After gaining the trust of the wild Braixen, you are now met with 3 different options.
1. Pet the Braixen
2. Hug the Braixen
3. Kiss the Braixen

The goal is to heighten your friendship level to 100.
Firsty, pet the Braixen. you can only pet the Braixen 8 times until they decide to move away. once this happens, hug the Braixen and you will then start Chapter 1 of the story!
(Note: Don't try and kiss the Braixen. they'll bonk you on the head and you'll lose 10 health points.)
